First 90 days in RPI (practical plan)

Click to expand
  • Stakeholder listening tour (RPI leadership, research admin, key units).
  • Inventory dashboards, datasets, refresh schedules, and pain points.
  • Map request intake process (what’s working, what’s missing, where approvals stall).
  • Quick wins: fix broken refreshes, align definitions, document assumptions.
  • Define KPI dictionary and reporting standards (naming, lineage, validation checks).
  • Implement intake workflow: triage, priority scoring, SLAs, status transparency.
  • Identify top 2–3 strategic insight initiatives with measurable outcomes.
  • Establish recurring “Insights Review” cadence with leadership (short, actionable briefs).
  • Build a prioritized roadmap for RPI analytics (platform, dashboards, process).
  • Pilot explanatory/predictive monitoring where it helps (capacity, cycle times, bottlenecks).
  • Strengthen collaboration patterns: documentation, handoffs, “definition of done.”
  • Track adoption and impact (usage, time saved, decision outcomes).

Questions for the committee

Signals strategic fit

  • How are analytics requests prioritized today, and who owns final prioritization?
  • Which systems are the primary sources for research operations data (and how mature is integration)?
