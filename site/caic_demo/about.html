About CAIC

Building AI that puts people first

We're a nonprofit consortium on a mission to ensure that AI development in our region is inclusive, ethical, and grounded in the real needs of our community.

🎯

Our Mission

To catalyze inclusive, human-centered, responsible AI to improve the lives of people in the Cincinnati region — and beyond. We provide coordinated, collective AI capabilities that align with ethical principles and serve all members of our community.

🔭

Our Vision

A Cincinnati region where artificial intelligence is developed and deployed responsibly — benefiting everyone, reducing inequality, and serving as a national model for inclusive, ethical AI adoption in mid-sized American cities.

What We Do

Coordinated. Collective. Responsible.

CAIC brings together organizations, researchers, practitioners, and community members to advance AI responsibly across multiple sectors.

🤝

Convene & Connect

We bring together diverse stakeholders — from government to education to healthcare — to collaborate on shared AI challenges and opportunities.

📚

Educate & Inform

Through our newsletter, events, and resources, we raise AI literacy across the region and help communities understand both the promise and the risks of AI.

⚙️

Build & Deploy

Our project teams develop practical, responsible AI applications in areas where they can make the greatest positive impact on community wellbeing.

🛡️

Advocate & Advise

We advise policymakers, organizations, and institutions on responsible AI governance, helping shape frameworks that protect community interests.

🔬

Research & Evaluate

Through our Research, Risk & Responsible AI team, we analyze emerging AI trends and assess impacts to inform decision-making across all our initiatives.

🌱

Grow & Sustain

We build lasting infrastructure for responsible AI in Cincinnati — growing membership, partnerships, and resources to ensure long-term impact.

Key Initiative

The AI Blueprint for Cincinnati

Our flagship strategic document, the AI Blueprint for Cincinnati, provides a comprehensive framework for how our region should approach AI development, deployment, and governance.

The blueprint addresses:

  • ✓ Regional AI governance and policy frameworks
  • ✓ Workforce transition and skills development
  • ✓ Equity and inclusion in AI access and benefit
  • ✓ Ethical deployment standards for organizations
  • ✓ Community engagement and public trust-building
