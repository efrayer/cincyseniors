Project Teams

AI working across every sector

Seven focused teams tackling responsible AI challenges across the domains that shape daily life in the Cincinnati region. Find your fit and join the work.

🎓
Education

AI in Education

K-12, higher education, and vocational programs

Join This Team

Our AI in Education team works with schools, teachers, students, and administrators across K-12, higher education, and vocational training to responsibly integrate AI tools that improve outcomes without exacerbating inequity.

K-12 Schools

Supporting educators with responsible AI tools for personalized learning, administrative efficiency, and student support.

Higher Education

Partnering with colleges and universities on AI policy, academic integrity frameworks, and research applications.

Vocational Training

Preparing trade and vocational students for AI-augmented workplaces with practical, hands-on curriculum.

AI Literacy

Building foundational AI understanding across all grade levels to prepare students for an AI-shaped future.

🏛️
Government

AI in Government

Civic AI, public services, and policy

Join This Team

Our Government team helps local and regional government agencies understand, evaluate, and responsibly deploy AI to improve public services — while ensuring citizen rights, privacy, and accountability are protected.

Public Services

Using AI to streamline permit processing, constituent services, and government operations while maintaining transparency.

Civic Engagement

Exploring AI tools that increase citizen participation and make government more accessible to all residents.

AI Policy Development

Advising city and county officials on AI governance frameworks, procurement standards, and ethical guidelines.

Public Safety

Critically examining AI in law enforcement and emergency services with strong civil liberties protections.

🏥
Healthcare

AI in Healthcare

Clinical AI, health equity, and medical research

Join This Team

Cincinnati's world-class healthcare system provides a unique opportunity to advance clinical AI responsibly. Our team connects healthcare providers, researchers, and patients to ensure AI improves care quality and health equity.

Clinical Applications

Evaluating AI diagnostic tools, clinical decision support, and predictive analytics with rigorous safety standards.

Health Equity

Ensuring AI tools don't encode existing disparities — working to close gaps in care access and outcomes.

Patient Privacy

Advocating for strong data protection standards and informed consent in AI-powered healthcare systems.

Medical Research

Supporting AI-accelerated research while maintaining rigorous ethical standards and open science principles.

💛
Non-Profits

AI for Non-Profits

Empowering mission-driven organizations

Join This Team

Nonprofits are on the front lines of community need but often lack resources to adopt powerful new tools. Our team helps mission-driven organizations leverage AI responsibly to amplify their impact without compromising their values.

Capacity Building

Hands-on workshops and consulting to help nonprofits evaluate, adopt, and use AI tools effectively.

Fundraising & Operations

AI tools for grant writing, donor engagement, volunteer coordination, and administrative efficiency.

Program Delivery

Using AI to personalize services, improve outcomes measurement, and extend reach to underserved populations.

Ethical Guidance

Helping nonprofits maintain integrity and community trust when deploying AI-powered programs and tools.

👴
Older Adults

AI for Older Adults

Accessibility, aging, and digital inclusion

Join This Team

Older adults represent one of the fastest-growing demographic groups yet are often left out of AI conversations. Our team works to ensure AI development addresses the unique needs, concerns, and contributions of seniors.

Accessibility

Advocating for AI systems designed with older adults in mind — voice interfaces, simplified UX, and assistive technologies.

Elder Care

Responsible AI for home care, monitoring, medication management, and social connection for aging populations.

Digital Literacy

Education programs helping older adults confidently and safely navigate AI-powered devices and services.

Fraud Prevention

Addressing AI-enabled scams, disinformation, and exploitation targeting seniors with protective tools and education.

💼
Workforce

Workforce Development

Skills, transitions, and the future of work

Join This Team

AI is transforming the job market. Our Workforce Development team helps workers, employers, and training institutions navigate this transition — building skills, creating pathways, and ensuring no one is left behind.

Upskilling Programs

Building AI literacy and practical skills for workers across industries facing automation and transformation.

Career Transitions

Supporting workers moving from declining roles into AI-adjacent careers with retraining and placement support.

Employer Partnerships

Connecting businesses with talent pipelines and helping employers responsibly implement AI in the workplace.

Policy Advocacy

Advocating for worker protections, safety nets, and equitable AI deployment standards with regional employers.

🔬
Research

Research, Risk & Responsible AI

Ethics, policy, and AI governance

Join This Team

The foundational team that informs all CAIC work. We research AI trends, assess risks, develop ethical frameworks, and build the governance infrastructure that ensures all CAIC initiatives meet our responsible AI standards.

AI Risk Assessment

Evaluating emerging AI systems for bias, safety risks, and potential negative societal impacts across all domains.

Ethics Frameworks

Developing practical ethical guidelines and decision-making tools for organizations deploying AI systems.

Policy Research

Tracking and analyzing AI legislation, regulation, and policy at local, state, federal, and international levels.

Community Research

Studying AI's real-world impacts on Cincinnati communities and using findings to shape CAIC programming.
