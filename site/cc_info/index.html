Who We Are

Mission, Vision & Values

Understanding our foundational identity helps every team member connect their daily work to the larger purpose of the organization.

Our Mission

To serve people through God's love in their times of vulnerability — providing help, creating hope, and building community across Southwestern Ohio.

Our Vision

A community where all people experience dignity, justice, and compassion, and have the resources they need to thrive.

Our Tagline

Serve. Engage. Empower.

Core Values

What Guides Our Work

  • ☤
    Faith Grounded in Catholic social teaching and the belief in the dignity of every person
  • 🤝
    Compassion Meeting people where they are, with empathy and without judgment
  • 🌟
    Dignity Honoring the inherent worth of every individual we serve
  • ⚖
    Excellence Committed to high-quality, accredited services and continuous improvement
  • 🏠
    Community Building connections and belonging across our 11-county service region
What We Do

Our Programs

CCSWOH operates 10 distinct programs addressing a wide range of human needs — from food access and mental health to immigration support and senior companionship.

🍽
Food Access
Food for All

Provides food assistance and nutritional support to individuals and families facing food insecurity across the region.

  • Emergency food pantry access
  • Nutrition education and resources
  • Community food partnerships
View Program ↗
🧘
Mental Health
Mental Health Services

Licensed counseling and behavioral health services for individuals, children, and families — including early childhood mental health consultation.

  • Individual & family counseling
  • Early Childhood Mental Health Consultation
  • Intake: (513) 489-8898
View Program ↗
💼
Workforce
Employment Services

Job readiness, training, and placement support for job seekers, plus workforce solutions for employer partners.

  • Resume & interview preparation
  • Job placement assistance
  • Employer partnership program
View Program ↗
⚖
Legal Services
Immigration Legal Services

Accredited legal representation and counsel for immigrants navigating U.S. immigration law and processes.

  • Visa & green card applications
  • Citizenship / naturalization
  • DACA & humanitarian cases
View Program ↗
🌎
Refugee & Migrant
Refugee Resettlement Services

Comprehensive support for refugees and migrants as they rebuild their lives — housing, employment, language, and cultural integration.

  • Initial resettlement & housing
  • Cultural orientation & case management
  • Path to self-sufficiency
View Program ↗
🧑
Family Support
Family Development Services

Comprehensive family support, parenting education, and post-adoption services to strengthen families at every stage.

  • Parenting education classes
  • Post Adoption support
  • Family stability resources
View Program ↗
👴
Senior Services
Caregiver Assistance Network

Support services and resources for family caregivers who are caring for elderly or disabled loved ones at home.

  • Respite care coordination
  • Caregiver coaching & education
  • Community referral support
View Program ↗
🎭
Senior Volunteers
AmeriCorps Seniors Programs

Two federally funded programs placing senior volunteers to mentor youth (Foster Grandparent) and companion isolated older adults (Senior Companion).

  • Foster Grandparent Program
  • Senior Companion Program
  • Non-medical companionship for seniors
View Programs ↗

Hispanic Community
Su Casa Hispanic Center

A dedicated community hub providing culturally responsive services, ESOL classes, and support for Hispanic families in the region.

  • ESOL / English language classes
  • Case management for immigrant families
  • Phone: (513) 761-1588

Language Access
AccuracyNow Language Services

Professional interpretation and translation services supporting multilingual communication for clients, staff, and partner agencies.

  • In-person & remote interpretation

Accreditations & Recognitions
