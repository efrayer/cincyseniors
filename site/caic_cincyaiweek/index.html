Our Mission

AI designed to work for everyone

The Cincinnati AI Catalyst (CAIC) is a nonprofit consortium dedicated to catalyzing inclusive, human-centered, responsible AI — harnessing the benefits of AI while minimizing risks and negative societal impacts across the Cincinnati region and beyond.

🤝

Inclusive by Design

We ensure AI solutions reach and benefit all communities, especially underserved populations.

⚖️

Responsible AI Principles

Every initiative is grounded in ethical frameworks that prioritize fairness, transparency, and accountability.

🌎

Community-Centered Impact

Our work is driven by the real needs of Cincinnati residents — not just technological possibility.

What's happening during AI Week

Panels, workshops, demos, and community conversations — across seven days and multiple venues.

OCT 10

Opening Keynote & Welcome Reception

Kick off Cincinnati AI Week with an inspiring keynote on the future of responsible AI in our region — followed by a networking reception open to all.

🕕 6:00 PM EST 📍 Cincinnati, OH Keynote
OCT 11

AI in Education Day — Tools for Teachers & Students

Workshops and sessions for K-12 educators, higher education faculty, and students on responsible AI integration in the classroom.

🕙 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM 📍 Hybrid Workshop Day
OCT 13

Responsible AI in Healthcare — Panel Discussion

Leading clinicians and AI researchers discuss ethical AI deployment in Cincinnati's world-class healthcare system.

🕔 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM 📍 Cincinnati, OH Panel
OCT 15

AI & the Future of Work — Workforce Development Summit

Practical sessions for workers, job-seekers, and employers on navigating the AI-transformed economy with skills, equity, and opportunity.

🕙 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM 📍 Cincinnati, OH Summit
OCT 16

Closing Celebration & Community Showcase

A showcase of CAIC team projects and local AI initiatives — celebrating a week of learning, connecting, and building toward a better AI future for Cincinnati.

🕔 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM 📍 Cincinnati, OH Showcase
