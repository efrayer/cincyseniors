Plain-language summary: CincySeniors.org is a free, community-run educational website. We collect only what we need to serve you, we never sell your data, and you can ask us to delete your information at any time. The full details are below.

1. Who We Are

CincySeniors.org is operated by the Cincy AI Catalyst (CAIC), a community initiative dedicated to helping older adults in the Greater Cincinnati region learn about and confidently use artificial intelligence tools. We are a non-commercial, community-run project.

Data Controller: Cincy AI Catalyst (CAIC)
Contact: info@cincyseniors.org
Website: CincySeniors.org

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you fill out a form on our site (newsletter signup, volunteer interest, Town Hall or small group registration), we collect:

  • Required: First name, last name, and email address
  • Optional: Age range and county / part of town

You are never required to provide optional information, and leaving those fields blank will not affect your ability to sign up.

Information collected automatically

When you visit CincySeniors.org, our web hosting provider may automatically collect standard technical information, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • The website that referred you to us
  • Date and time of your visit

This information is used solely to maintain and improve the website and is not linked to your personal identity.

Information from Cindy AI

When you use our Cindy AI chat assistant, the text of your conversation is sent to our AI processing service to generate a response. We do not store the content of individual chat conversations long-term. Please do not share sensitive personal information (such as Social Security numbers, financial account details, or medical record numbers) in the chat.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To send you the monthly CincySeniors newsletter (if you subscribed)
  • To contact you about volunteer opportunities you expressed interest in
  • To notify you when a Town Hall or small group session is scheduled near you
  • To understand the geographic needs of our community so we can plan events appropriately
  • To maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the website

We will never use your information for advertising, profiling, automated decision-making, or any purpose not listed above.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information — ever.

We share your information only with the following trusted service providers, strictly for the purposes described above:

  • Formspree (formspree.io): Processes and delivers form submissions to our team email. Formspree stores submissions on its servers temporarily. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Web hosting provider: Hosts the website files and serves them to your browser.

We may also disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

CincySeniors.org uses a small number of cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your experience:

  • Text size preference: We store your chosen text size (Normal, Large, Largest) in your browser's localStorage so it is remembered on future visits. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.
  • Cookie consent: We store a small cookie to remember that you have acknowledged our cookie notice, so we don't show it to you on every visit.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. If you clear your browser's cookies and local storage, your preferences will be reset on your next visit.

By clicking "OK" on the cookie notice, or by continuing to use this site, you consent to our use of these limited cookies as described above.

6. How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain form submission data (name, email, and any optional fields) for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe. You may unsubscribe at any time by replying to any newsletter email.
  • Volunteer interest: Until you ask us to remove you, or until our volunteer program ends.
  • Event registrations: Until the relevant event season has concluded, typically no longer than 12 months.

You may request deletion of your information at any time — see Section 7 below.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your information from our records
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for example, by unsubscribing from the newsletter)
  • Object to our processing of your information
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at info@cincyseniors.org with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All form submissions are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Our service providers are selected in part for their security practices.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

9. AI Content Disclaimer

The Cindy AI chat assistant on this website is designed to provide general information about AI tools and local senior resources in Greater Cincinnati. Please be aware:

  • Cindy AI may not have complete or current information about every local service or program.
  • Responses are generated by an AI model and may occasionally be inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Always confirm important details — such as hours, eligibility, or availability — directly with the service provider.
  • Cindy AI does not provide medical, legal, or financial advice. For such matters, please consult a qualified professional.
  • For emergencies, call 911. For crisis support, call or text 988.

10. Children's Privacy

CincySeniors.org is intended for adults aged 60 and older, their families, and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us at info@cincyseniors.org and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of CincySeniors.org after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. How to Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out:

  • Email: info@cincyseniors.org
  • Website: CincySeniors.org

We are happy to answer any questions and will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

13. Definitions

Term Definition
Personal Data Any information that can identify you directly or indirectly, such as your name or email address.
Usage Data Information collected automatically when you visit the site, such as browser type, pages visited, and time of visit.
User Any individual who accesses or uses CincySeniors.org.
Cookie A small file stored on your device by your browser that helps websites remember your preferences.
Data Controller The organization responsible for deciding how personal data is collected and used — in this case, Cincy AI Catalyst (CAIC).
Service Provider A third-party company that processes data on our behalf, such as Formspree for form submissions.
