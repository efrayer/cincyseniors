Volunteer with Us

Share your time and patience. Volunteers sit alongside seniors — in libraries, senior centers, and community spaces — to help them feel confident using AI tools.

What volunteering looks like

  • Attend a Town Hall or small group session as a helper — not a teacher
  • Answer questions one-on-one in plain, patient language
  • Help participants try AI tools hands-on at their own speed
  • No advanced tech skills required — just friendliness and good listening
  • Flexible commitment — help when you're available, wherever you are in Greater Cincinnati
