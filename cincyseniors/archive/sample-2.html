How we keep you safe

Read our policies

01

Your privacy

We don't ask for your name, address, or phone number to read our guides. When you ask our helper a question, your conversation is not sold or shared — period.

02

Honest answers

Every guide is reviewed by a human before publishing. If we don't know something, we say so — and point you to someone who does.

03

Never for sale

No ads. No sponsored posts. No "preferred partners." Funded by local organizations and modest reader contributions.

Ask a question

How our helper works

Start with a search. Type a question in plain English — like "is this Medicare text real" or "how do I write a thank-you note" — and we'll point you to the right guide.

Example: "I got a call saying my grandson is in trouble. What should I do?"

Or talk to our helper

A plain-English chat, ready when you are.

It can explain a word, summarize a letter, or help you draft a reply. If your question needs a real person, it will say so.
